ABOUT HOTEL NIKA

Book Your Room & Enjoy The Comfort

Located in Vlorë, a few steps from Radhimë Beach, Hotel Nika Radhime has accommodations with a restaurant, free private parking, a garden and a bar. Offering a private beach area, the property is located within a 15-minute walk of Al Breeze Beach. The hotel also provides free WiFi as well as a paid airport shuttle service.

Sophisticated Comfort



At the hotel, the rooms include a closet. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and slippers, the rooms at Hotel Nika Radhime have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and some rooms contain a balcony. All guest rooms have bed linen. Kevi Beach is 1.5 miles from the accommodation, while Independence Square is 8.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 101 miles from Hotel Nika Radhime.
